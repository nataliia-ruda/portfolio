About me

I am a full-stack web developer with nearly two years of experience and a strong passion for technology and coding. I enjoy creating creative, functional, and responsive web applications that deliver a great user experience using modern web technologies.
What motivates me most is the ability to solve real-life problems and improve everyday workflows through code, transforming ideas into practical digital solutions.
My biggest achievement so far is DuckTrack, a job applications tracker that I designed, built, and deployed independently. The project solves a real-world problem by helping job seekers stay organized and motivated in today’s challenging job market. After publishing it on several social platforms, it attracted my first real active users, which was a highly motivating milestone in my developer journey.
